INFORMATION

Studio Policies

Deposit Requirements

A non-refundable $100 deposit is required to secure your appointment and begin the design process. This deposit is applied toward your final session cost.

Cancellation Policy

We require 72 hours notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations will forfeit the deposit and require a new deposit to reschedule.

Pricing

Hourly Rate

$200/hour with a 3-hour minimum for new clients. Large-scale pieces may be quoted as a flat rate.

Design Fees

Custom design work is included in the hourly rate. Complex designs requiring extensive drawing time may require additional design fees.

Aftercare

Healing Process

Proper healing takes 2-4 weeks. You'll receive detailed aftercare instructions and recommended products after your session.

Touch-Ups

Minor touch-ups within 3 months of your session are complimentary. Significant changes or additions will be charged at the standard rate.
