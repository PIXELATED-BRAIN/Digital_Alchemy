Our Story

Founded in 2003, AMA Shipping began as a small regional delivery service with just 3 vehicles and a dedicated team of 10 professionals. Today, we've grown into a global logistics powerhouse with operations in over 150 countries.

Our journey has been fueled by a commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction. We've continuously adapted to the changing landscape of global trade, investing in cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices.

What sets us apart is our people-first approach. We believe that our employees are our greatest asset, and their dedication to excellence is what drives our success.
