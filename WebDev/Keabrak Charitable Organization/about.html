About Us

Keabrak Charitable Organization is an Ethiopian indigenous, non-governmental and nonprofit humanitarian organization which was founded by two dedicated individuals in 2023 leading by board members and registered in accordance with civil society’s organization proclamation number 1113/2019 as Local Organization with Registry Number 7010.

The Organization is dedicated to serve the community and build inclusive societies in which all women, men, girls, and boys are valued and empowered to make choices about their own development. We are engaged to work in different thematic areas like peace building, women’s economic empowerment, gender-based violence and inclusive public services including education, health, and nutrition. We are passionate about what we do and value professional excellence, respect and integrity, partnerships, trust, and the people we work with.


Vision


"A society where every individual has equal opportunities, basic needs are met, and poverty is eradicated, fostering sustainable and inclusive communities."



Mission


  • Create enabling opportunities for women, youth and mothers collaborating with responsible body.
  • Work in coordination with governmental and non-governmental organizations concerned with how to ensure women's economic empowerment and economic freedom.
  • Provide holistic support by putting children in a center.
  • Provide capacity building trainings to various segments of society.


Core Values

  1. EMPOWERMENT:Empowering women to become agents of change.
  2. EQUALITY:Striving for equal rights and opportunities for all.
  3. INCLUSIVITY:Ensuring diversity in all programs and services.
  4. COLLABORATION:Fostering partnerships for collective impact.
  5. EDUCATION AND SKILL DEVELOPMENT:Prioritizing education as a tool for empowerment.
  6. ADVOCACY:Advocating for gender equality and women’s rights.
